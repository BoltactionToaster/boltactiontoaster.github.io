About

I am a passionate software engineer and a graduate of the University of Central Florida's computer science program. I started programming in high school to learn to make games. From there, I had decided that I really enjoyed programming, though much of what I learned was game development focused until university. However, once in university, I was exposed to many different fields of software development. From web development to systems software, operating systems, and even machine learning. I learned so much about the inner workings of many underlying systems the modern world runs off of and takes for granted. I'm ready to continue learning what I need to be successful in my career and reach my goals.
